What We Do at Madame Potato

Here at Madame Potato, our potato kits taste good, makes you feel good from the best sustainably-sourced local produce. We work with farmers who are committed to farming sustainably and work with partners who are excited by our mission who sell our potato kits.

When you buy Madame Potato kits, we want you to be reassured that your potatoes have been grown pesticide-free and in a way that is friendly to the surrounding wild animals and forests. We work with farmers to ensure we can stock the best seasonally appropriate potatoes. Simply pop the kit into the microwave for the set amount of time and enjoy your fresh jacket potato! All of our packaging is sustainable and recyclable, so you can eat well and treat our planet well.
