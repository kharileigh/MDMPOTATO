What We Do at Madame Potato

Here at Madame Potato, our potatoes taste good, makes you feel good from the best sustainably-sourced local produce. We work with farmers who are committed to farming sustainably and work with partners who are excited by our mission who sell our produce.

When you buy Madame Potatoes, we want you to be reassured that your potatoes have been grown pesticide-free and in a way that is friendly to the surrounding wild animals and forests. We work with farmers to ensure we can stock the best seasonally appropriate potatoes.
